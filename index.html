Our Problem Statement

Ideal

An easy-to-use system for collecting data as part of the Smoke and CO Alarm Program in Kawartha Lakes. Providing data collection with discrete address selection, tracking and mapping functions for firefighters conducting this program.

Reality

The current process involves firefighters going door-to-door and collecting data on paper. Afterwards results are transferred to a master tracking sheet.

Consequences

The current process is time-consuming when firefighters visit homes that do not need an inspection and record keeping can be a lengthy process. Another downfall is that paper tabulation allows for the possibility of losing data. As a result, the effectiveness of the programs' purpose is affected.

Proposal

Create an app to select discrete addresses and collect data for the Smoke and CO Alarm Program. Map component would include residences that have "opted out" of inspection, and residences that have been completed.
