Project Progress
Week 1
  • The group was formed and brainstorming began
  • A problem statement including a proposal for an app was created
  • The team reached out to our contact at the Kawartha Lakes Fire Department for further information and project recommendations
  • Individual members each researched and experimented with various softwares and methods for achieving the project goals
  • An ArcGIS online group was created to allow for sharing of data and ideas
  • A test layer containing point data and addresses was created in ArcMap for team members to experiment with
Week 2
  • Parcel data of Lindsay containing addresses was acquired through Ontario GeoHub and Land Information Ontario (LIO)
  • After technical review, the team began to focus on using Esri products Collector and Survey123
  • The data was converted from polygon features to point features and added to the ArcGIS online group for use in Collector
  • The client responded with details regarding the form their Fire Fighters currently use to collect data by pen and paper
  • Using the information provided by the client, a survey was created in Survey123
  • Team members started to design the website layout to display the final project
Week 3
  • The website layout was finalized and content added
  • Additional map layers were created and published to the ArcGIS online group for use in Collector
  • The Survey form was finalized and linked to the map in Collector
  • A video was made demonstrating how to use the app
